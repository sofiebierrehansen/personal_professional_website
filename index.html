Who am I?

My life has always been split between my inherent interest for the non-boundary and creative and my structural approach to life. During my studies as a multimedia designer at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology (KEA), I've come to realize that these two do not have to be each other’s opposites as I've been given the tools to express myself creatively through structure in the field of digital design.

My design passion lies in the small things that create the bigger value. I have an eye for detail and the patience to back it up which has been the common denominator in my projects throughout my studies. I am user oriented, but I also realize that the client might not always be able to express what they want. This is where I have the ability to create solutions together with the client in order to not only achieve a satisfactory result but rather a complete, user-friendly solution.

Personally, I am what I call a "colorful minimalist". I like colors and what feelings they can induce in people, but also prefer simplicity in my design expression. Although I do enjoy challenging myself with other design styles depending on the case and purpose.

During my studies at KEA, I’ve come to realize which challenges lie within the field of digital design and where I can have an impact on these. I’m therefore more than ever eager to apply my skills but also develop these further and gain even more in the field of digital design, UI design, prototyping and graphic design.
